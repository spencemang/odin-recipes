Fancy Boy Chicken Casserole

Description

The best, fanciest chicken casserole I ever had. I can only have it once a year, but I bet the mob lawyer my wife ran off with has it ALL the time.

Ingredients

  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 30 buttery round crackers (such as Ritz®), crushed
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • teaspoon sliced scallions, or to taste
  • 1 ½ teaspoons Creole seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder, divided
  • 5 cups shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 1 cup finely chopped yellow onion
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
